# Open Collective Funding for 0pnMatrx

## What Is Open Collective?

Open Collective is a transparent funding platform that lets open-source projects collect and spend money in full public view. Every dollar received and every dollar spent is visible on the project's Open Collective dashboard. There are no backroom deals, no hidden accounts, no ambiguity about where the money goes.

For 0pnMatrx this is not optional philosophy -- it is core infrastructure. The project exists to build a free, open-source AI agent platform for the people. That mission falls apart the moment funding becomes opaque. Open Collective guarantees that every contributor, sponsor, and user can audit exactly how the project sustains itself.

Key benefits:

- **Full financial transparency.** All income and expenses are published in real time on the Open Collective dashboard.
- **Fiscal hosting.** Open Source Collective (a 501(c)(6) organization) acts as fiscal host, handling legal entity status, tax compliance, and banking so the project team can focus on building.
- **Tax receipts for sponsors.** Corporate sponsors receive proper invoices and tax-deductible receipts through the fiscal host.
- **No vendor lock-in.** The project owns its collective and can migrate fiscal hosts if needed.
- **Community trust.** Contributors can see that their donations and sponsorships directly fund the work they care about.

## Corporate Sponsor Tiers

Corporate sponsorships provide predictable, recurring revenue that funds long-term development. Each tier includes invoicing and tax receipts issued through Open Source Collective.

### Bronze Corporate -- $500/month

- Company logo displayed in the project README (sponsors section)
- Named acknowledgment in the CHANGELOG for each release during the sponsorship period
- Invoice and tax receipt provided monthly through Open Source Collective
- Ideal for companies that use 0pnMatrx internally and want to support continued development

### Silver Corporate -- $1,000/month

- Everything in Bronze
- Company logo displayed on the 0pnMatrx landing page
- Monthly developer update call (30 minutes) with a core maintainer covering roadmap progress, upcoming features, and known issues
- Invoice and tax receipt provided monthly through Open Source Collective
- Ideal for companies building products on top of 0pnMatrx that need to stay ahead of changes

### Gold Corporate -- $2,500/month

- Everything in Silver
- Dedicated integration support: direct access to a maintainer for troubleshooting integration issues, reviewing pull requests that affect your use case, and advising on architecture decisions
- Roadmap influence: your feature requests and priorities are formally considered during quarterly planning, and you receive written feedback on how they were evaluated
- Invoice and tax receipt provided monthly through Open Source Collective
- Ideal for companies whose core product depends on 0pnMatrx and who need stability guarantees

### Platinum Corporate -- $5,000/month

- Everything in Gold
- White-label rights: permission to rebrand and redistribute 0pnMatrx under your own product name, subject to the white-label addendum (contact maintainers for details)
- 10 Enterprise licenses included free of charge (normally sold through Gumroad), covering premium features, priority support, and SLA guarantees
- Quarterly strategic review with the project lead covering long-term direction, partnership opportunities, and ecosystem alignment
- Invoice and tax receipt provided monthly through Open Source Collective
- Ideal for organizations that want to build an entire product line on the 0pnMatrx platform

## How Funds Are Spent

All expenses are published on the Open Collective dashboard. The target allocation is:

| Category | Allocation | What It Covers |
|---|---|---|
| Infrastructure and Hosting | 40% | Cloud servers, CI/CD pipelines, domain registrations, monitoring services, blockchain node costs, IPFS pinning, database hosting |
| Development | 30% | New feature implementation, bug fixes, security patches, code reviews, dependency updates, performance optimization |
| iOS App Maintenance | 20% | App Store submissions, TestFlight builds, Swift/SwiftUI updates, device testing, Apple Developer Program fees, UI/UX improvements |
| Community Programs and Grants | 10% | Contributor bounties, hackathon sponsorships, documentation sprints, onboarding materials, travel grants for meetups and conferences |

These percentages are targets, not rigid constraints. The actual split may vary quarter to quarter based on project needs. Any significant deviation (more than 5 percentage points from target) will be explained in a quarterly transparency report posted to the repository.

## How to Contribute

### As an Individual

Individual contributions at any amount are welcome and deeply appreciated. Every dollar directly funds the work listed above.

1. Go to [https://opencollective.com/openmatrix](https://opencollective.com/openmatrix)
2. Click "Contribute"
3. Choose a one-time or recurring contribution at any amount
4. Optionally leave a public message that will appear on the contributors page

Individual contributors receive:

- Public recognition on the Open Collective contributors page
- A warm feeling of directly funding open-source infrastructure that matters
- For recurring contributors ($10/month or more): named acknowledgment in quarterly transparency reports

### As a Corporate Sponsor

Corporate sponsorships provide invoices, tax receipts, and the visibility benefits described in the tier structure above.

1. Go to [https://opencollective.com/openmatrix](https://opencollective.com/openmatrix)
2. Select the corporate tier that matches your needs (Bronze, Silver, Gold, or Platinum)
3. Complete the contribution using your company payment method
4. You will receive an invoice and tax receipt from Open Source Collective
5. For Silver tier and above, email the maintainers at the address listed on the collective page to schedule your first update call

If you need a custom sponsorship arrangement, a different billing cycle, or have questions about the tiers, open an issue on the GitHub repository or contact the maintainers directly through Open Collective.

## Transparency Commitment

Financial transparency is not a feature of 0pnMatrx -- it is a requirement. The project commits to:

- **Real-time visibility.** All income and expenses are visible on the Open Collective dashboard at [https://opencollective.com/openmatrix](https://opencollective.com/openmatrix) as they happen. Nothing is hidden.
- **Quarterly reports.** A written summary of income, expenses, and allocation breakdown is posted to the repository every quarter.
- **Open decision-making.** Major spending decisions (any single expense over $500) are discussed in a public GitHub issue before being submitted.
- **Audit trail.** Every expense submitted to Open Collective includes a description, receipt, and category. Anyone can review these at any time.
- **No private funds.** The project does not maintain any private bank accounts or payment channels for project-related income. All project funding flows through Open Collective.

This is what it means to build for the people. The money is as open as the code.
