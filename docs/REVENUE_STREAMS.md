## 1. Pro Subscriptions

**Price:** $4.99/month
**Type:** Recurring (Stripe)
**Target:** Power users, small businesses, developers

Raises all free-tier limits significantly: 100 contract conversions/month,
50 NFT mints, $500k DeFi volume, 120 API calls/min, dashboard export,
custom agent skills, and early access to new features.

**Expected at 1,000 users:** ~100 Pro subscribers = $499/month

## 2. Enterprise Subscriptions

**Price:** $19.99/month
**Type:** Recurring (Stripe)
**Target:** Teams, agencies, enterprises

Removes all limits. Adds team accounts, white-label branding, audit log
export, direct API access, priority support, and plugin submission rights.

**Expected at 1,000 users:** ~20 Enterprise subscribers = $400/month

## 3. Glasswing Security Audits

**Price:** $299 (Standard) / $599 (Advanced) / $999 (Enterprise)
**Type:** One-time per contract (Stripe)
**Target:** Any project deploying smart contracts

Automated 12-point vulnerability scanning with PDF reports. Enterprise
tier includes manual review and a first-year Glasswing Security Badge.

**Expected at 1,000 users:** ~10 audits/month avg $299 = $2,990/month

## 4. Solidity Template Packs (Gumroad)

**Price:** $49 each / $119 bundle
**Type:** One-time digital product
**Target:** Developers building DeFi, creator economy, or business apps

Three packs of 5 production-ready Solidity contracts each (15 total).
Sold via Gumroad. No ongoing cost to the platform.

**Expected at 1,000 users:** ~10 purchases/month avg $49 = $490/month

## 5. Plugin Marketplace

**Split:** Developers keep 90%, platform takes 10%
**Type:** Revenue share (Stripe)
**Target:** Developer ecosystem

Third-party developers sell plugins. Free plugins are always welcome.
Revenue scales with ecosystem growth.

**Expected at 1,000 users:** Depends on ecosystem maturity. Early: minimal.
At scale with 50+ paid plugins: potentially $500-2,000/month.

## 6. GitHub Sponsors

**Tiers:** $5 / $25 / $100 / $500 / $2,500 per month
**Type:** Donation (GitHub)
**Target:** Individual developers and companies who benefit from the platform

Keeps the free tier free forever. Sponsors get recognition, community
access, and influence on the roadmap proportional to their tier.

**Expected at 1,000 users:** Variable. Comparable OSS projects: $200-2,000/month.

## 7. Referral Program

**Model:** Free subscription credits for referrers
**Type:** Growth mechanism (reduces customer acquisition cost)
**Target:** Existing users

Referrers earn 1 month free per Pro referral, 2 months per Enterprise
referral. Referred users get an extended trial. This is a cost centre,
not a revenue stream — it reduces CAC and increases LTV.

**Impact at 1,000 users:** Estimated 15-25% of new paid subscribers
arrive via referral, reducing marketing spend.

## 8. Metered API Pricing

**Tiers:** Growth ($49.99/mo + overage) / Scale ($199.99/mo) / Infrastructure ($499.99/mo)
**Type:** Usage-based recurring (Stripe)
**Target:** Startups and companies building on the API

For high-volume API consumers who exceed Enterprise limits. Includes
generous included calls with per-call overage billing.

**Expected at 1,000 users:** Early: 2-5 metered customers = $100-2,500/month.
At scale: significant.

## 9. Protocol Referral Fees

**Model:** Integrator fees from DeFi protocols (Uniswap 25bps, Aave referral, 1inch referral)
**Type:** Automatic, percentage of transaction volume
**Target:** All DeFi transactions routed through the platform

Many DeFi protocols pay integrators who route transactions through them.
0pnMatrx collects these fees automatically and routes them to NeoSafe.
Only active when blockchain is deployed.

**Expected at 1,000 users:** Depends on DeFi volume. At $1M monthly swap
volume: ~$2,500/month from Uniswap fees alone.
