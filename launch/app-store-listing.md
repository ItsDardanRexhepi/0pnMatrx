## Description

Meet MTRX -- the simplest way to do powerful things on the blockchain.

Whether you have never touched crypto or you run a portfolio worth millions, MTRX gives you three AI agents that handle every detail for you. No jargon, no gas fees, no guesswork.

YOUR THREE AGENTS

Trinity is your conversational interface. Ask her anything in plain language -- "Send $200 to my sister in Lagos," "What is my portfolio doing this week?" -- and she translates your words into on-chain action. She remembers your preferences, explains things clearly, and never makes you feel lost.

Neo is the execution engine behind the scenes. He builds and deploys smart contracts, mints NFTs, manages liquidity positions, and carries out complex DeFi strategies -- all in seconds. You describe what you want; Neo makes it happen on Base, Ethereum's fastest Layer 2 network.

Morpheus steps in when the stakes are high. Before you approve a large transaction, enter a new protocol, or restructure a DAO, Morpheus walks you through the risks, models the outcomes, and makes sure you understand exactly what you are signing.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

Move money anywhere in the world. MTRX handles cross-border payments, currency conversion, and peer-to-peer transfers with near-zero cost and near-instant settlement.

Grow your assets. Lend and borrow through DeFi protocols, stake tokens for yield, provide liquidity, and let the agents optimize your positions automatically.

Build and own digital assets. Mint NFTs from your camera roll, convert real-world agreements into smart contracts, and manage tokenized assets -- no coding required.

Govern and collaborate. Create or join a DAO, vote on proposals, manage a shared treasury, and coordinate with your community directly from the app.

Stay protected. Access on-chain insurance products, set spending limits, create multi-signature wallets, and get real-time alerts when something looks wrong.

COMPLETELY FREE TO START

The Free tier gives you full access to all 30 blockchain services with zero gas fees. We cover every transaction cost through an ERC-4337 paymaster -- you never pay gas, period. Upgrade when you are ready, or stay on Free forever.

SUBSCRIPTION TIERS

Free -- $0/month. All 30 services, zero gas fees, Trinity conversational access.

Pro -- $4.99/month. Everything in Free plus priority execution through Neo, advanced portfolio analytics, higher transaction limits, and Morpheus risk analysis on every action. Start with a 3-day free trial.

Enterprise -- $19.99/month. Everything in Pro plus dedicated agent instances, API access for team integrations, custom smart contract templates, DAO administration tools, and white-glove onboarding. Start with a 3-day free trial.

PRIVACY FIRST

Your data stays yours. Biometric authentication and on-device processing keep sensitive information off our servers. We never sell your data. Our backend is MIT-licensed open source -- anyone can audit exactly how MTRX works.

Built by Dardan Rexhepi. Launching May 21, 2026.
