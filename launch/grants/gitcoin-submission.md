## Field: Description

0pnMatrx is a free, open-source platform that lets anyone access blockchain financial services through simple conversation. No wallet setup. No gas fees. No technical knowledge. Just tell the AI what you need, and it handles everything.

Think of it as having a personal financial advisor who can also execute smart contracts, send money across borders, set up insurance, manage investments, and protect your creative work -- all for free, all on-chain, all open source.

**30 blockchain services** running on Base. **3 AI agents** that understand plain English. **329 tests** passing in CI. **Gas-free transactions** through an ERC-4337 paymaster. **MTRX iOS app** launching May 21, 2026. **MIT licensed** -- free forever, no token, no paywall.

One person built this in 40 days.

1.7 billion people have no bank account. Blockchain was supposed to fix this. But using blockchain still requires understanding seed phrases, gas fees, and contract ABIs. The people who need decentralized finance the most cannot access it.

0pnMatrx fixes that. You talk to Trinity. She handles the rest.

## Field: Why this deserves matching

0pnMatrx is a public good in the most literal sense:

- **MIT licensed.** Anyone can use it, fork it, modify it, or redistribute it.
- **No token.** No governance token, no utility token, no value extraction from users.
- **Free by default.** Ollama runs locally and costs nothing. No API key required.
- **Gas sponsored.** Users never pay gas. The platform absorbs this cost.
- **Solo built.** One builder, 40 days, 30 services, 329 tests. Every donated dollar goes further because overhead is zero.

This is the kind of project quadratic funding was designed for: a genuine public good that serves a large number of people, built by an individual without corporate backing, and available to everyone without restriction.
