Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers to common questions

Is Free really free? +
Yes, genuinely free forever. No credit card required. The Free tier covers all core functionality including all 30 blockchain services and all three AI agents. You get real usage limits, not a crippled demo. Most individual users never need to upgrade.
What are platform fees? +
Small on-chain fees are applied to certain blockchain transactions. These fees route to the NeoSafe multisig wallet and fund ongoing platform development, infrastructure costs, and security audits. All fees are fully transparent and visible on-chain -- you can verify every transaction yourself.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Cancel from your account settings at any time. You keep full access to your current tier until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees, no retention flows, no tricks. One click and you are done.
What happens to my data if I downgrade? +
Your data is never deleted. You retain full read access to everything you created -- contracts, audit reports, conversation history, configurations. Usage limits simply reset to the free tier levels. If you upgrade again later, everything is still there.
Do I need a wallet? +
No. You can use the platform without a wallet for basic features like contract conversion previews, chat, and documentation. Wallet connection is only needed when you want to execute on-chain actions like deploying contracts, minting NFTs, or participating in governance.
📜

Platform Fee Disclosure

0pnMatrx applies small platform fees on certain on-chain transactions (typically 0.3-1%). These fees are routed directly to the NeoSafe multisig treasury, which is publicly auditable on-chain. Funds cover infrastructure, security audits, and ongoing development. Subscription pricing covers off-chain features like increased rate limits, team management, and priority support. We believe in honest, predictable pricing with no hidden costs.
